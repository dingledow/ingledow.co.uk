A Study on Female Mate Choice

The information provided in the following study is for use in an undergraduate anthropology dissertation at Durham University.

In the following questions you will be asked for information relating to your previous sexual experiences as well as your attitudes to sex generally. All information provided is completely anonymous, confidential and cannot be traced back to you. You are free to withdraw from the study at any time.

By clicking continue you confirm that you are female, aged 18 or over, agree that you have read and understood all of the above information and consent for the information you provide to be used in our study.

If you have any further questions relating to the study or would like to be notified of its findings please do not hesitate to contact me.
