SEMI PRECIOUS MAGAZINE

ABOUT

Semi Precious Magazine is a magazine concept that targets young, professional adults that are interested in Politics, Culture, Style and Technology. The aim of the magazine is to make sense of a contradictory world for it's readers.

BRANDING AND LOGO DESIGN

Like the target readers of Semi Precious Magazine, the branding is sophisticated.

WEB DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT

The website needed to link visitors to the articles. This was achieved by showing the latest articles on the home page by using javascript effects. The design of the website has taken inspiration from art directed blogs, where each article is uniquely designed in layout and style to complement the content of the story.
