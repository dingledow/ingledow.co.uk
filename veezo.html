Veezo is an iOS application that intelligently manages their media content and adapts to their everyday routine. It intelligently recommends content based on the time, but also based on the users likes, dislikes and history.

Veezo allows users to catalogue the best content in their own personal collections.

It also allows users to easily share their favourite content with their friends and family.

The brief was introduced by explaining the problems that users encounter when managing media on different devices.<.p>

It asked us to consider how we could design an interface and service that could create a meaningful dialogue with two target audiences: Organised Parents and the Young Tech Elite.

Through research of existing services and of users, we highlighted problems including the difficulty of sharing and quickly accessing meaningful content for the user.

Using post it notes to explain methods of interaction.

Iterating designs in the form of paper prototypes.
